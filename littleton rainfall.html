About the Data
All precipitation from the KAPA (Centennial Airport) ASOS tipping-bucket gauge via Iowa State's IEM archive — same sensor as MHFD ALERT and CSU precip maps. Real hourly readings, no models.

Forecast is the official NWS 7-day for the Littleton grid point.

Annual avg: 17.5" per NOAA normals for Littleton/south Denver (Denver airport averages 14.3" — drier, east of the foothills).
