About the Data
KAPA Airport — ASOS tipping-bucket gauge at Centennial Airport (~3 mi east), daily totals summed from hourly readings via Iowa State IEM archive.

CO-AR-164 — CoCoRaHS volunteer gauge at Centennial 3.7 W, near your home. Daily readings reported once per morning. Active since Nov 2005, 97% coverage.

Note: CoCoRaHS reports lag by ~1 day (observer reads gauge each morning). Today's CO-AR-164 reading won't appear until tomorrow.

Forecast: Official NWS 7-day for your grid point.
